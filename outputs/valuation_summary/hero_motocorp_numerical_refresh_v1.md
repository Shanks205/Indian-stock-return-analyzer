## Interpretation

The preliminary numerical model is intentionally conservative. It does **not** mean the stock is automatically a sell or avoid. It means that under the current v1 assumptions, the DCF does not yet justify the prevailing market price.

Before drawing a final investment conclusion, the following must be refreshed:

- audited FY26 annual report
- actual FY26 revenue, EBIT, tax, capex, and working capital
- share count
- cash and debt reconciliation
- beta regression
- peer valuation table
- sensitivity table review
- latest market price

## Analyst note

This result is valuable because it prevents thesis bias. The business may be strong, but the DCF must still ask whether the current market price already discounts most of the good news.

The correct conclusion is:

```text
Hero MotoCorp remains a strong research case, but the conservative v1 DCF requires audited-data refresh before any valuation call is finalized.
```
