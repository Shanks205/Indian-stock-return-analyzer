# Hindustan Zinc Peer Framework v1

## Purpose

This file completes the peer-comparison layer for the Hindustan Zinc public-source v1 research pack.

Hindustan Zinc should not be compared mechanically with every metals company because its zinc-lead-silver mix, high-margin mining base, dividend profile, and Vedanta ownership structure differ from diversified metals companies.

Peer comparison should be used as a valuation sanity check, not as a replacement for cycle-normalized DCF.

## Interpretation framework

### Hindustan Zinc vs Vedanta

Vedanta is relevant because of ownership and diversified metals exposure, but Vedanta has broader commodity exposure and a different leverage/capital-structure profile. It should not mechanically anchor Hindustan Zinc's valuation.

### Hindustan Zinc vs NALCO

NALCO is useful as a commodity and PSU-style comparison, but aluminium/alumina economics differ from zinc/lead/silver economics.

### Hindustan Zinc vs Hindalco

Hindalco offers broader metals-cycle context. However, its global downstream business and aluminium exposure make it an imperfect direct comparable.

### Hindustan Zinc vs NMDC

NMDC is a mining PSU reference. It helps frame mining-cycle and public-sector valuation, but product exposure is different.

### Hindustan Zinc vs Coal India

Coal India is a PSU commodity/dividend reference. It should not be used as a direct valuation anchor because commodity exposure and operating economics are different.
