# Triveni Turbine Peer Framework v1

## Purpose

This file completes the peer-comparison layer for the Triveni Turbine public-source v1 research pack.

Triveni Turbine should not be compared mechanically with every capital-goods company because its niche in industrial steam turbines, export orientation, order-book profile, aftermarket potential and high margin structure differ from broader electrical or infrastructure equipment players.

Peer comparison should be used as a valuation sanity check, not as a replacement for normalized DCF and order-book conversion analysis.

## Interpretation framework

### Triveni Turbine vs Thermax

Thermax is a useful energy and industrial equipment comparison, but its diversified project, services and environment portfolio differs from Triveni's more focused turbine business.

### Triveni Turbine vs Siemens and ABB India

Siemens and ABB are premium capital-goods benchmarks. They help frame quality and valuation expectations, but their scale, diversification and multinational parentage make them imperfect direct peers.

### Triveni Turbine vs TD Power Systems

TD Power Systems is useful for export-oriented capital-goods comparison, especially where order book, export growth and margin quality are central debates.

### Triveni Turbine vs Kirloskar Brothers and Bharat Bijlee

These companies provide broader industrial equipment context. Their product mix and project exposure differ, so multiples should be interpreted cautiously.
