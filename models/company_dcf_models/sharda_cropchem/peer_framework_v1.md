# Sharda Cropchem Peer Framework v1

## Purpose

This file completes the peer-comparison layer for the Sharda Cropchem public-source v1 research pack.

Sharda Cropchem should not be compared mechanically with every chemical company because its asset-light, registration-led, globally distributed agrochemical model differs from manufacturing-heavy chemical businesses and domestic-only crop-protection companies.

Peer comparison should be used as a valuation sanity check, not as a replacement for normalized DCF and working-capital analysis.

## Interpretation framework

### Sharda Cropchem vs UPL

UPL is a global agrochemical reference, but its scale, leverage profile, manufacturing and business complexity differ significantly. It is useful for agrochemical-cycle context, not a direct valuation anchor.

### Sharda Cropchem vs PI Industries

PI Industries is a high-quality Indian agrochemical / CSM benchmark. It may command a different multiple because of stronger quality perception, CSM visibility and margin profile.

### Sharda Cropchem vs Sumitomo Chemical India

Sumitomo Chemical India offers crop-protection comparison, but its parentage, domestic mix and product portfolio differ.

### Sharda Cropchem vs Rallis India and Dhanuka Agritech

These companies provide useful domestic agrochemical context. However, Sharda Cropchem has higher global/export exposure and a registration-led model.

### Sharda Cropchem vs Bharat Rasayan

Bharat Rasayan provides a manufacturing-heavy agrochemical reference, but Sharda Cropchem's asset-light model means capex, margins and working capital should be interpreted differently.
